
# Problem Solving Model

##  Traditional Life Cycle/Water Fall 
Waterfall is an organized way of solving problem. Its called a life cycle as a projector will go through all stages of it.
Its a sequential process not an iterative, spiral or cyclic. Sequential mean one task end and another one starts.

### Why its called Waterfall life cycle?
There are many alternates of waterfall cycle.
Flow of process is one after another like water going down. If we see the slide , we will see it cascading down.
Imagine you are a fish and you are to develop a project. You are at a stage. you finish the current stage. You will go through each step not skipping one.
Similarly if we have to go to back a few steps lets say because customer added some new requirement. Just like a fish , it will be impossible for you to go back the waterfall. Just like 
How a fish cant go back stream. So if we go in the direction of waterfall. its easy and not hard. but if we have to go backward its so hard.
### The good thing
- You can show your progress through the project.
- It is  a structured Approach.
- Have specialized people only for the stage working that stage.
### The bad
- Take more time
- Cant go to previous stage.
- Take more resources.
