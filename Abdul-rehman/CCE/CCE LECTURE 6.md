# Advocacy and Activism
How do these terms bring in social  and political change?
	They bring change by taking a stand against some stance to right something that is wrong.
		Help Communities raise their voice
## Advocacy
To take a stand for some purpose or  a cause.
It is to promote an issue through an organized effort. Through unity we change the mind of other people for our cause.
It influences laws and decision-maker.
### Objectives of acdvocacy
- It spreads awareness and shapes public policy.
- It works for long-term improvement.
### Types of advocacy
- It can be policy , legal , media , or community focused.
## Activism
Is how you make your cause or purpose reach the right place

### Objectives of activism
- Activism creates urgency and mobilize support.
