### Three pillars: State , Government , Civil Society

#### What is the difference between state and government?
Government is a machinery it contains all the machinery to run a state. A state is a political identity. Such as the Islamic Republic of Pakistan.  A state is permanent , it includes territory , population, sovereignty. Government is temporary changes through elections or revolution
#### State 
A State is a political organization with authority over a defined territory and population. It is recognized both *internally* and *Externally*.

#####  Essential Elements Of a state
1. Population
2. Territory
	1. Defined Premises
3. Government
4. Sovereignty
	1. The power and Authority
##### Function Of a state
- Maintain Law And Order
-  Provide Security and defense
- Economic and social welfare
- Protect citizen Right

#### Government
The machine through which the will of the state is enforced.
##### Forms of Government
- Democracy
- Monarchy
- Dictatorship
##### Organs Of Government 
- Legislature - Makes Laws
- Executive - Enforce Law
- Judiciary - Interpret Law
	- Judiciary interprets law, see if the law is against the people or do discrimination against people

#### Civil Society 
Sphere of voluntary association and organization between family and state.
##### Examples of Civil Society
- NGOS.
- Media.
- Trade Unions.
- Community.
- Groups.
- Student Union.
- Religious Organization.
##### Functions of Civil Society
- Provides Citizen Participation.
- Ensure Accountability.
- Protects Minority Right.
- Promotes Trust and cooperation.
