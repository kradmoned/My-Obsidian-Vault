## Civic Rights and Duties , Ethics , Civic Engagement

## Rights and responsibilities
- Rights are legal moral or social entitlements
- Responsibilities are your duties
## Fundamental Rights
- Guaranteed to all citizens
- Protect freedom, Equality and dignity
- Categories include equality, freedom , life , religion, property , education.
## Right To Equality
- Equality before law
- Non discrimination
- Equal opportunities for all
## Right To Freedom
- Freedom of Movement.
- Freedom of Assembly and Association.
- Freedom of speech , press, and information.
## Right to Life and Fair Trial
- Right to life and liberty.
- Right to Fair trial.
- Protection of law and dignity of man .
## Right to Religion and Property
- Freedom of religion
- Right to own and protect property.
## Right to Education Obedience.
- Free and compulsory education for children aged 5-16.
## Responsibilities of citizen.
- Article: 5 Loyalty to state.
- Obedience to the constitution and law.
- Pay taxes honestly.
- Defend Pakistan when required
- Protect public property and environment.
## Civic Rights
- Right to vote.
- Right to access public service.
- Right to political Participation.
## Civic Duties
- Casting votes responsibility.
- Following laws and traffic rules.
- Participating in community service.
- Respecting other rights.
