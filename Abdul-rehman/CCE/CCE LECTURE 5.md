# Community Engagement: Concept , Nature & Approaches

## What is a Community
A group of people living together or sharing common interest
Example: Villages, students, online gaming groups

## Nature of community
- Social in nature- people interact and share.
- Dynamic - Changes over time.
- Provide Identity and belonging.
## Characteristic of community
- Common interest(Religion, culture)
	- christian people living in the same place
- Shared Space(Geography)
- Cooperation and mutual support
- Rules traditions and values
	- Maybe people their are not nosy
	- Maybe you agree with their traditions and values
## What is community Engagement?
- Working with people , not jut FOR them
	- You do such work that benefits you as well as them, such as planting flowers in the community as a group effort
- Listening , involving, and empoewering
	- Things like instead of waiting for the government do the work of community urself

- Identify local needs
- Involve citizens in decision making
- Empower marginalized voices
- Create sense of ownership

- Planned Efforts to improve social and economic life.
- Example: Skills training programs for unemployed Youth
